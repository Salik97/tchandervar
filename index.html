Letter from ‘tchāndērvār’

‘tchandervar’ began with a question: what if art didn’t wait to be visited?
It is an invitation — to witness stillness, to host beauty, and to live with art that listens.
This edition brings together works that deserve to rest not only in galleries, but in spaces that breathe.

Art with Purpose

tchandervar connects local artists with individuals who want to enhance their spaces with unique, high-quality artwork. We curate a diverse collection of paintings and other art forms, carefully chosen to fit a variety of environments. Clients can explore this collection or collaborate with us to find pieces that best suit their space, whether it's a residence, a hotel, office, or restaurant.

Our service goes beyond just selling art — we provide personalized recommendations to create the perfect ambiance for each setting. Once an individual selects a piece, we handle the entire process, from facilitating the purchase to managing logistics like delivery and installation.

In addition to providing beautiful art, tchandervar helps clients support local artists, fostering a vibrant creative community.
