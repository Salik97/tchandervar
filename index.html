Letter from ‘tchāndērvār’

‘tchandervar’ began with a question: what if art didn’t wait to be visited?
It is an invitation — to witness stillness, to host beauty, and to live with art that listens. This edition brings together works that deserve to rest not only in galleries, but in spaces that breathe.
