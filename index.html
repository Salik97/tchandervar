Our customized frames are designed to enhance—never overpower the delicate rhythm of art. Drawing inspiration from the harmony found in Kashmiri crafts, architecture, and landscape, each frame brings a quiet reverence to the artwork it surrounds.

Each frame is crafted to feel like an extension of the work—not a decoration, but a continuation. Whether it's a soft gold border, a textured neutral surface, or a deep matte space that lets the artwork breathe.

Art with Purpose

tchāndērvār connects local artists with individuals who want to enhance their spaces with unique, high-quality artwork. We curate a diverse collection of paintings and other art forms, carefully chosen to fit a variety of environments.

Clients can explore this collection or collaborate with us to find pieces that best suit their space—whether it's a residence, a hotel, office, or restaurant. Our service goes beyond just selling art—we provide personalized recommendations to create the perfect ambiance for each setting.

Once an individual selects a piece, we handle the entire process—from facilitating the purchase to managing logistics like delivery and installation. In addition to providing beautiful art, tchāndērvār helps clients support local artists, fostering a vibrant creative community.
